Input a number into the text box and click the button to add mario entities.
Every time a Mario comes into contact with another mario, one or both will die and disappear.
If one lives he will gain a small amount of additional jumping power.
Marios spawn at random x locations and have a semi-random jumping height to start.
With large numbers of Marios added, some might disappear immediately.
Don't be frightened. It's not a bug. It's a feature!!
